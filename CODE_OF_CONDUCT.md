# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in HumaneProxy a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviour includes:**

- Being kind, empathetic, and respectful
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project's mission

**Unacceptable behaviour includes:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sexualised language or imagery
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Our Mission

HumaneProxy exists to **protect human lives**. We ask all contributors to keep this mission at heart. Discussions about self-harm detection, crisis resources, and safety classification should be handled with sensitivity and care.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project maintainer at **mishra@vishisht.tech**. All complaints will be reviewed and responded to promptly.

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and to temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behaviour they deem inappropriate.
